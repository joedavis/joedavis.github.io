As of today the blog runs on Hakyll rather than Jekyll, something I’ve been meaning to do for a while. Now all of my writing can be done using Pandoc.

Seeing as I haven’t exactly had any traffic to the blog, and there hasn’t been much worth reading, I didn’t see much of an issue in allowing this change.
